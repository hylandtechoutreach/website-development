# 2.09: Final Projects
For this lesson, students will spend the whole time working on their final projects. There are no specific learning objectives; the goal is for students to strengthen their foundation of understanding, and demonstrate their skills.

## Accommodation
If a student does not want to present a final project in class, they are welcome to submit a video. If they are unable to submit a video, they can simply submit the project itself. If they would like another alternative, they can take a summative assessment (test) to ensure they have retained all of the skills they learned in the course.
